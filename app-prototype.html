TransitEase App Prototype

**TransitEase** is a user-friendly app designed to make commuting more efficient. By offering real-time transit data, customizable travel alerts, and route planning, TransitEase simplifies the way you travel and helps you save time on your daily commute.

Key Features

  • Real-time transit data to keep you updated on bus and train schedules.
  • Customizable travel alerts for specific routes or stops.
  • Interactive route planning to optimize your daily commute.
  • GPS tracking to monitor your journey and adjust your schedule in real time.
  • Integration with calendar apps to set reminders for your trips.

How It Works

Using **TransitEase** is easy. Simply open the app and allow location access to receive real-time data on your nearest transit options.
